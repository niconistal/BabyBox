Why BabyBox?

Designed for tiny hands, loved by parents

A media player that puts kids in control of their entertainment, while giving parents peace of mind.

RFID Magic

Each figurine has a hidden RFID tag. Place it on the box, and the assigned content plays instantly. No buttons, no screens.

Screen Time Limits

Set daily video limits by count or duration. LEDs warn when time's almost up. Audio is always unlimited.

Parent Dashboard

A friendly web interface to download content from YouTube, assign figurines, and manage settings from your phone.

Simple as 1-2-3

How it works

From YouTube to your toddler's hands in three easy steps.

1

Download

Paste a YouTube link in the parent dashboard. Choose video or audio-only.

2

Assign

Scan a figurine and link it to the downloaded content. Done in seconds.

3

Place & Play

Your child places the figurine on the box. Content plays instantly. Magic!

Screen time control

Smart video limits that kids understand

Set daily video limits and let the LED strip do the talking. No arguments, no negotiations.

Yellow warning

LEDs turn yellow when the daily limit is almost reached, giving a gentle heads-up.

Red stop

LEDs flash red when the limit is reached. No more videos today, but audio keeps playing!

Audio unlimited

Music and audio content are never limited. Kids can always listen to their favorites.
