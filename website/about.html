About BabyBox

The story behind the toddler-friendly media player

The Story

BabyBox was born from a simple frustration: handing a toddler a tablet just to play a song. Screens are designed for adults — unlock, navigate, tap the right button, avoid the wrong ad. A toddler shouldn't need to do any of that.

We wanted something physical, something tangible. Place a figurine, hear a song. That's it. No accounts, no algorithms, no in-app purchases. Just a child's favorite character playing their favorite music.

Open Source

BabyBox is completely open source — hardware designs, software, and 3D models. We believe every family should be able to build one without paying for subscriptions or proprietary hardware.

The entire project is available on GitHub. Fork it, modify it, make it yours. Contributions are welcome.
