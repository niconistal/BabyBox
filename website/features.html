Features

Everything BabyBox Can Do

From RFID detection to parental controls, every feature is designed to be simple for kids and powerful for parents.

RFID Figurine Detection

The MFRC522 reader detects RFID tags instantly when a figurine is placed on the box. No pairing, no delay -- just place and play.

Magnetic Snap Placement

Neodymium magnets in both the figurine and the box top guide the figurine into perfect alignment over the RFID antenna every time.

Playback Lock

Once content starts playing, placing a new figurine does nothing. This prevents accidental interruptions and teaches kids to finish what they started.

Video Limits

Set daily video limits by count or total duration, configurable per day of the week. Audio content is never limited -- only screen time is managed.

LED Animations

The WS2812 RGB strip communicates visually: a rainbow sweep on scan, pulsing while playing, yellow warning near limits, and red when done.

Buzzer Feedback

A passive buzzer on GPIO 12 plays distinct tones for scan success, errors, and limit warnings -- audio cues kids learn to recognize instantly.

Bluetooth Speaker

All audio routes to a paired Bluetooth speaker via PulseAudio. Video goes to HDMI for the TV, audio goes to the speaker -- best of both worlds.

Parent Dashboard

A Flask-powered web UI accessible from any device on your network. Manage content, assign figurines, set limits, and view history -- all from your phone.

YouTube Download

Paste any YouTube URL in the dashboard, choose video or audio-only, and yt-dlp handles the rest. Title, duration, and thumbnail are auto-populated.

Figurine Registration

Scan any RFID tag and assign it to downloaded content in seconds. The dashboard walks you through it -- tap the figurine, pick the content, done.

Playback History

Track what was played and when. See which figurines are favorites, how much video time was used, and review activity from the parent dashboard.
